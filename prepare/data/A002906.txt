(m is the magnetic moment of a single spin; this factor may be present or absent depending on the precise definition of the susceptibility.) The b-file has been obtained from the series by Guttmann and Jensen via the substitution t = v/(1-v^2). - _Andrey Zabolotskiy_, Feb 11 2022"
