    "formula": [
        "a(n) = Sum_{s} 2^M(s)/Product_{i} i^s(i)*s(i)!, where the sum is over n-tuples s in [0..n]^n such that n = Sum i*s(i), M(s) = Sum_{i<j} s(i)*s(j)*gcd(i,j) + Sum_{i} (s(i)*(floor(i/2) - 1) + i*binomial(s(i),2)) + sign(Sum_{k} s(2*k+1)). [Robinson's formula, from Mallows & Sloane, simplified.] - _M. F. Hasler_, Apr 15 2012; corrected by _Sean A. Irvine_, Nov 05 2014"
    ],
